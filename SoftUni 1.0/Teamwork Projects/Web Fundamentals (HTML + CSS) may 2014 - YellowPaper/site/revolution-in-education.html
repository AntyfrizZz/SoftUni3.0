Sve Nakovsen: "I am revolutionizing the world of education!"

Everyone, throughout Bulgaria, has heard about the Software University, his founder Sve Nakovsen and his mission to create quality education for future badass programmers. In every interview, he explains how bad and outdated the education in the country is, promoting his idea for making a revolution in the sphere. But what are the boundaries of the revolution Nakovsen is talking about so passionately? How can we be sure this revolution will limit to the sphere of software knowledge?

The average Bulgarian might be fooled with all these sweet talks about bright future, quality knowledge and Silicon Valley on the Balkans but our team of professional journalists is on the track of something more alarming and wicked than you might imagine. Only couple of months of investigation was enough for our undercover agents with code names kaka Sofka and bati Neycho to find out the real mission of the university. Below are their stories:

Kaka Sofka: The Software University literally wants to rule the world of education. Sve Nakovsen and his team plan to occupy all universities in Sofia thus cut all possibilities for disorientated high school graduates to have access to low quality education. This is unthinkable!!! What about our right to be dumb and become dumber?!

Bati Neycho: Furthermore, they plan to exile all professors and their assistants to some of the remotest villages in Bulgaria where they will grow vegetables and fruit to feed the growing army of Softuni Students.
