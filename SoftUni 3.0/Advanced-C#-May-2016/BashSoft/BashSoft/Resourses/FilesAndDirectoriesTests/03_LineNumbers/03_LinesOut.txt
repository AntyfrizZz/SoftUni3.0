1. Coffee is slightly acidic and can have a 
2. stimulating effect on humans because of 
3. its caffeine content. Coffee is one of the 
4. most popular drinks in the world.[3] It 
5. can be prepared and presented in a variety
6. of ways (e.g., espresso, cappuccino, 
7. cafe latte, etc.). It is usually served
8. hot, although iced coffee is also served.
9. Clinical studies indicate that moderate 
10. coffee consumption is benign or mildly 
11. beneficial in healthy adults, with continuing 
12. research on whether long-term consumption 
13. inhibits cognitive decline during aging or 
14. lowers the risk of some forms of cancer.
